Welcome Message

Welcome to my SECIP 2025 ePortfolio! Over two intensive and enriching weeks, I had the incredible opportunity to explore social innovation through hands-on teamwork, design thinking, and cross-cultural collaboration. This site documents my reflections, learnings, and personal growth throughout the program.

About the SECIP Program

The Southeast Asia Social Innovation and Cultural Immersion Program (SECIP 2025) brings together students from various countries to collaboratively address real-world challenges through the lens of social entrepreneurship and innovation. The program focuses on building empathy, applying theory to practice, and creating impactful solutions across cultural contexts.

Purpose of this ePortfolio

This ePortfolio is a reflection of my journey, combining individual and team experiences. It includes three main entries:

  • Week 1 Reflection – Understanding social problems, team formation, and early-stage ideation.
  • Week 2 Reflection – Prototyping solutions, pitching ideas, and overcoming challenges.
  • Final Reflection – My overall takeaways, growth, and future aspirations.

Each section highlights what I’ve learned, contributed, and experienced during SECIP 2025.
